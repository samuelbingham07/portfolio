About me

I'm Sam, an undergrad student at Pomona College studying Cognitive Science with concentrations in AI and Human-Centered Design. I'm currently seeking roles in product management and product design, primarily the tech industry (but also in the gaming industry :D).

I love designing and collaborating--my favorite project is my small business, JPNLAvintage, which I designed from the ground up and have big plans for (self-code the website first and foremost, make an AI to automate the vintage product dating process, and grow my Japanese market). On campus, I am President of the 5C Nikkei Student Union, fellow-ship recipient of Product Space, PM of P-ai, member of club basketball, and liaison of my major's department.

Outside of work and school, I love backpacking solo around the world (Instagram: @samandhisbackpack), playing video games (my favorite is Hades I/II) and developing them too (check out my first game, Mumei), and expanding my cocktail repertoire.
