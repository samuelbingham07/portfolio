JPNLAvintage

Check out the Website Showcase and Behind The Scenes!

JPNLAvintage is my vintage clothing brand turned online small business, where I curate pieces sourced between Japan and Los Angeles. I run everything from inventory sourcing and photography to pricing, social media, and customer support. 200+ products are already up, and there are hundreds more ready to be listed!

Through this project I’ve learned a ton about:

  • Entrepreneurship and small business operations
  • Ecommerce strategy & marketplace dynamics
  • Social media marketing & competitor analysis
  • UI/UX for vintage clothing & ecommerce
  • Branding, visual identity, and copywriting
