Terms of Service

Solo project  |  Cooperative dungeon crawler  |  Players: 4  |  ~45 min

Terms of Service is a solo cooperative dungeon crawler I made for Advanced Game Design, designed as an art game about tech platform dark patterns. Four players sit down to what looks like a normal dungeon crawl, but every few rooms a System Update card interrupts the game and the rules change mid-session. Coins get introduced without explanation, ads start appearing on tiles, and by the end there's a premium tier offering to let you pay to skip them. By the time you win, you've agreed to everything.

It comes directly from my background in product management: I've read enough monetization roadmaps to recognize the pattern, and the real ToS is the legal infrastructure that makes all of it possible. I wanted to make something that let people feel it rather than just explain it.

Production

I designed three dungeon tilesets in Aseprite, all the System Update and action cards, the coin economy, and the physical rulebook in Adobe Illustrator, with everything printed and fabricated at the Harvey Mudd Makerspace and Scripps Fab Lab.
