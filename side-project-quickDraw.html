QuickDraw

Check out the web version below! Play QuickDraw

QuickDraw is a fully functioning bullet-hell game built in just 3 days for the 2026 Western Game Jam, following the themes of Western and Everything Goes Wrong. Every mechanic, sprite, and level was designed and coded from scratch under the jam's time constraint.

Through this project I've learned and practiced:

  • Godot engine and GDScript
  • Bullet-hell game design and balancing
  • 2D pixel art with Aseprite
  • Rapid prototyping and scoping under a deadline
