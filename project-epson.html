Epson: Marcomm LATAM Intern

Product Marketing Management, LATAM
June–August 2026 (8 weeks) · Hybrid, Epson Headquarters, Los Alamitos, CA

I'm interning as the Marcomm LATAM Intern on Epson's Product Marketing Management team, supporting marketing operations across three product lines: System Devices (SD), SIDM (Serial Impact Dot Matrix printers), and ColorWorks (on-demand color label printers). The role sits at the intersection of Product Management, Marcom, and Web, keeping the marketing content and tools that support LATAM sales aligned and current.

What I'm Working On

  • Partner Portal upkeep: Supporting the update and maintenance of the Partner Portal so product information, marketing materials, sales tools, and promotions stay accurate and current.
  • Marketing material reviews: Reviewing and updating datasheets, brochures, success stories, infographics, and sales presentations across the SD, ColorWorks, and SIDM lines.
  • Web and content audits: Conducting regular audits of the SD, ColorWorks, and SIDM websites and landing pages to confirm brochures, datasheets, videos, images, product specs, and syndicated content are published, current, and aligned with the marketing strategy.
  • Gap identification and coordination: Flagging content gaps, outdated materials, and inconsistencies, then coordinating the fixes with the Product Management, Marcom, and Web teams.
  • Regional marketing support: Providing administrative and operational support for regional marketing initiatives, events, and product launches.

Skills & Tools

  • Excel for tracking content audits and marketing data
  • SQL for pulling and querying product and sales data
  • Salesforce for marketing and sales coordination
  • Adobe for reviewing and editing marketing assets
  • Cross-functional coordination across Product Management, Marcom, and Web teams
  • Marketing content lifecycle management across multiple product lines and a regional (LATAM) market

I'm currently partway through this 8-week internship, more to add as the summer progresses!
