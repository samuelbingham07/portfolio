Haunting of the Whitcomb Manor

Team: Ari, Dante, Elisa, Sam  |  GM-less cooperative horror RPG  |  Players: 4

Haunting of the Whitcomb Manor is a GM-less cooperative horror RPG my team made for Advanced Game Design, built as an adaptation of Escape the Dark Castle. The four players are teenagers exploring an abandoned manor, working together without a game master to navigate chapter cards, fight enemies, and try to get everyone out alive and sane.

My Role

  • Narrative design: I wrote the chapter card story text, the premise, and the rulebook voice
  • Game mechanics: I designed the sanity tiering system, where losing sanity upgrades your dice and creates a risk/reward tension that ran through every encounter we built, as well as the collective turn-order structure and the resting mechanic
  • Visual design and fabrication: I handled the rulebook layout in Adobe Illustrator, hand-illustrated components, and all physical production at the Harvey Mudd Makerspace and Scripps Fab Lab
