Hobbies!

Here's what I do with my free time! Video games and board games are big ones but I don't have a way to show it off here.

Backpacking

Whenever I have a free couple weeks or more (and the budget), my favorite thing to do is explore a new country. Just me and my backpack, picking up phrases of new languages, trying new foods, and meeting new, different people. My favorite country out of my ~100 days of solo backpacking was Morocco, Germany, or Albania, and my favorite city was hands down Vienna. Tokyo and Japan will always be my all-time though!

My travel documentation insta!

Cocktails

My latest hobby is making cocktails--I'm starting by learning the basics and getting the classics down. Mojitos take the longest but they taste the best, so reconsider ordering one during a rush for the sake of your bartender!

Part-time Jobs

I've worked at a couple restaurants and bars before, but Gold Bar at Edition, a Marriott proprety in Tokyo was by far the best. My friends/coworkers were incredible and I will be visiting them, the customers (some celebrities) were fun, and I learned so much about hospitality and bartending from some of the best in the business.

Thrifting

Thrift stores are my third space. I love hunting for vintage clothes on weekends and curating my closet. My clothes lifecycle goes like this: thrift for $1 at the bins, wear until I find a similar piece I like a bit more, and sell the previous one for a deal at my flea markets.
