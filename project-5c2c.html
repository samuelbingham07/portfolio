5C2C

5C2C is a new marketplace built for the Claremont College Consortium that centers small businesses, student handcrafters, and service providers. The platform gives students a dedicated space to buy and sell within their own community. Launch Day soon!

Through this project I've learned and practiced:

  • Web design lead and frontend development
  • Marketplace UI/UX design principles
  • Community-centered product thinking
  • Collaboration within a student-run team
